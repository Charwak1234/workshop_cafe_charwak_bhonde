ABOUT US

Mission


Welcome to Aman cafe, where every cup tells a story and every bite brings joy! Nestled in the heart of Mumbai, our café is more than just a place to grab a quick coffee – it's a community hub where friends gather, ideas flow, and memories are made.

Founded in 1992 ,Aman Cafe began as a humble dream shared by Shatayu Balapure. Fueled by their passion for exceptional coffee and delicious food, they set out to create a space where people could escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and savor moments of tranquility. Over the years, our café has evolved into a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike. From our carefully curated menu featuring locally sourced ingredients to our cozy ambiance adorned with local art, every aspect of Aman Cafe reflects our commitment to quality and community..
